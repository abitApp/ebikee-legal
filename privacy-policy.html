Welcome to ebikee. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ebikee mobile application.

01 Information We Collect

When you use ebikee, we may collect:

  • Email address (for account authentication)
  • User ID
  • Messages sent within the app
  • Listings you create (including photos and descriptions)
  • Basic device information required for app functionality
We do not collect unnecessary personal data.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide account authentication (Magic Link or Google login)
  • Enable messaging between users
  • Display and manage listings
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send essential service-related emails (e.g., message notifications)
We do not sell your personal data.

03 Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase (database and authentication)
  • Google Authentication (if you choose Google login)
  • Resend (transactional email delivery)

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy and security standards.

04 Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account at any time. When your account is deleted, your personal data will be removed from our active systems.

For assistance, contact: support@ebikee.app

05 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

06 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
